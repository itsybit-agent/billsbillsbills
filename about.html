💸 BillsBillsBills

A private, local-first family expense tracker. No accounts, no cloud, no nonsense.

📋 What is this?

BillsBillsBills is a simple tool for tracking shared household expenses. Drop in your bank export, classify transactions by category, mark things as paid, and get a clear picture of where the money went.

Everything is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. Nothing leaves your device. No server, no login, no subscription.

✨ How it works

  • Drop an Excel export from your bank onto the app
  • Map the columns once — it remembers for next time
  • Auto-classifies transactions using rules you define
  • Anything without a rule lands in Other
  • Mark transactions as paid to track what's settled
  • Summary view breaks down spending by category and month
  • Export your data anytime as JSON or CSV backup

🔒 Privacy

Your financial data is sensitive. This app has no backend — everything lives in your browser's local storage. Export your data regularly to back it up or move it to another device.

🏗️ Built with

Vanilla JavaScript, event sourcing, and a healthy amount of frustration with existing expense trackers. Built by itsyBIT.

This is intentionally simple and opinionated — built for one family's workflow. If it doesn't quite fit yours, fork it and make it yours. That's the point.
